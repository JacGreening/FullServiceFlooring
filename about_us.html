Our Story

Three years ago, my partner, Alexa and I packed up our trailer and made the move to Tampa, where we currently live along with our two dogs and two cats. This vibrant city quickly became our new home, with its beautiful beaches and outdoor recreation. In our free time, you can find Alexa and I enjoying fishing and kayaking through the mangroves, or on the boardwalk in Saint Petersburg. These hobbies provide a perfect balance to my professional life and help you connect with the local community.


Growing up in central Wisconsin, I spent a significant amount of time working in flooring with my dad, which instilled in a strong work ethic and a passion for the trade. Drawing from that foundation, I eventually launched my own flooring company, and over time, I've successfully expanded my business and have been able to hire multiple employees. I have achieved a certification in luxury vinyl planking (LVP) from the International Certified Floorcovering Installers Association. I then went and earned my certification in Schluter Systems. My experience in both business and outdoor pursuits highlights your commitment to a well-rounded lifestyle in Tampa.
