Services Offered

What We Have To Offer Our Clients



Bathrooms

Transform your bathroom into a luxurious and functional space with our professional bathroom remodeling services! At Full Service Flooring, we specialize in creating personalized bathroom designs that match your style and meet your needs. Whether you're looking for a simple update or a complete renovation, we handle every aspect, from flooring and tiling to fixtures, lighting, and cabinetry.

  • Custom bathroom designs
  • High-quality flooring and tile installation
  • Vanity, sink, and countertop upgrades
  • Bathtub and shower remodeling
  • Plumbing and electrical work
  • Lighting and storage solutions

Flooring

Upgrade your home or business with top-quality flooring from Full Service Flooring We offer expert flooring installation services designed to meet your style, durability needs, and budget. Whether you're looking for hardwood, laminate, vinyl, tile, or carpet, our team will help you choose the perfect flooring solution to elevate your space.

  • Hardwood, laminate, and vinyl plank installation
  • Tile and stone flooring for bathrooms, kitchens, and more
  • Carpet installation for residential and commercial spaces
  • Flooring removal and disposal
  • Subfloor preparation and repairs

Kitchen

Bring your dream kitchen to life with Full Service Flooring's expert kitchen remodeling services! Whether you're looking for a modern upgrade or a complete transformation, we specialize in creating functional, stylish kitchens tailored to your needs and budget. From cabinetry and countertops to flooring and lighting, we handle every aspect of the remodel.

  • Custom kitchen designs
  • Cabinet installation and refinishing
  • High-quality countertops (granite, quartz, etc.)
  • Flooring: tile, hardwood, vinyl, and more
  • Appliance installation and upgrades
  • Lighting and storage solutions
  • Plumbing and electrical work
